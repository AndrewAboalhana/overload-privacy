Privacy Policy — Overload

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Overload ("the app") is a free, offline workout tracker developed by an individual developer. This privacy policy explains what data the app collects and how it is handled.

Data We Collect

Overload stores workout data (exercises, sets, reps, and weights) exclusively on your device using a local database. No data is transmitted to any server, and no account or sign-in is required.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, age, location)
  • No health or biometric data beyond what you manually enter
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No analytics or crash reports sent off-device
  • No cookies or tracking of any kind

Internet Access

Overload does not request the INTERNET permission. The app cannot connect to the internet and cannot send or receive any data over a network.

Data Export

The app includes an optional "Export Workout Data" feature that writes a CSV file to a location you choose on your own device using the Android Storage Access Framework. This export is performed entirely on-device and is not shared with the developer or any third party.

Notifications

On Android 13 and above, the app may request permission to send local notifications (for workout reminders). These notifications are generated locally and do not involve any external service.

Third-Party Services

Overload does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data services.

Data Deletion

All app data is stored on your device. You can delete all data at any time by uninstalling the app. There is no server-side data to request deletion of.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a revised "Last updated" date.
